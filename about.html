About Yogesh & Associates

Mumbai's trusted Chartered Accountant firm — building financial confidence since 2009

Our Story

15 Years of Excellence in Financial Services

Founded in 2009 by CA Ramesh Sharma, Yogesh & Associates began with a simple mission: provide honest, expert financial guidance that empowers individuals and businesses to thrive. What started as a boutique practice in Andheri has grown into one of Mumbai's most respected Chartered Accountancy firms, serving over 500 clients across India.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for proactive compliance, deep technical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to client success. Our team of qualified Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, and financial analysts work in unison to deliver comprehensive solutions across taxation, audit, business advisory, and accounting.

We are not merely your compliance partners — we are your strategic financial allies, dedicated to helping you navigate India's ever-evolving regulatory landscape with confidence and clarity.
