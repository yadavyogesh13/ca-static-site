Taxation Services

End-to-end tax compliance and strategic planning for individuals, HUFs, firms, LLPs, companies, and trusts

📄

Income Tax Filing (ITR)

Accurate preparation and e-filing of Income Tax Returns for all categories — individuals, HUFs, partnership firms, LLPs, companies, and trusts. We ensure you claim every legitimate deduction and exemption, minimising your tax liability while maintaining full compliance with the Income Tax Act, 1961.

ITR-1 to ITR-7 Revised Returns Belated Returns Refund Tracking Notice Response
🧾

GST Registration & Returns

From GST registration to monthly and quarterly return filing, we manage your complete GST compliance lifecycle — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and annual GSTR-9. We reconcile ITC mismatches, handle GST refunds, and respond to department notices on your behalf.

GST Registration GSTR-1, 3B, 9 ITC Reconciliation GST Refunds E-invoicing
💡

Tax Planning & Advisory

Strategic tax planning is an investment that pays for itself many times over. We analyse your financial position, income streams, and business structure to create a customised tax strategy that legally minimises your tax burden, optimises investments, and aligns with your long-term goals.

Tax Structuring Investment Planning HUF Formation Capital Gains Planning DTAA Advisory
✂️

TDS Compliance

We handle TDS deduction calculations, monthly challan payments, quarterly TDS return filing (24Q, 26Q, 27Q, 27EQ), issuance of Form 16/16A, and resolution of TDS defaults and demand notices from the Income Tax Department — with zero-delay guarantee.

24Q, 26Q, 27Q Form 16 & 16A TDS Corrections Lower Deduction Cert 26AS Reconciliation
📑

Audit & Assurance

Independent, objective audit services that provide assurance, detect risks, and strengthen financial governance

⚖️

Statutory Audit

Our statutory audit services comply with the Standards on Auditing (SAs) issued by ICAI. We conduct rigorous examination of financial statements, accounting records, and internal controls to provide an independent opinion on the true and fair view of financial position as required under the Companies Act 2013.

Companies Act 2013 ICAI Standards (SAs) CARO Reporting Management Letter Board Presentation
🔍

Internal Audit

Our risk-based internal audit services evaluate the adequacy of internal controls, identify process gaps, assess compliance with policies, and provide actionable recommendations to strengthen governance and operational effectiveness. We deliver quarterly reports aligned with best practices.

Risk-Based Audit Process Review Control Testing Quarterly Reports Audit Committee
📋

Tax Audit (Sec 44AB)

Tax audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act is mandatory for eligible businesses and professionals. We handle thorough examination of books of accounts, preparation of Form 3CA/3CB and Form 3CD with all required particulars, and e-filing within prescribed timelines.

Form 3CA / 3CB Form 3CD Sec 44AB Compliance Transfer Pricing
✅

Compliance Audit

Ensure your organisation adheres to all applicable laws and regulations. Our compliance audit covers corporate law, FEMA/RBI regulations, labour laws, and sector-specific requirements — providing a comprehensive compliance health check and remediation roadmap.

Corporate Law FEMA / RBI Labour Laws Secretarial Audit
🏢

Business Services

End-to-end business setup, registration, and corporate compliance to launch and grow your enterprise

🏛️

Company Registration

We handle the complete incorporation process — name approval with MCA, drafting of MOA and AOA, DIN and DSC applications, certificate of incorporation, PAN, TAN, opening bank account, and first-year compliance setup for Private Limited, One Person Company (OPC), or Public Limited companies.

Pvt Ltd / OPC MOA & AOA Drafting DIN / DSC / PAN MCA Filings Bank Account Setup
🤝

LLP Registration

A Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) offers partnership flexibility with limited liability protection. We manage the complete LLP formation — partner designations, LLP Agreement drafting and registration, and ongoing compliance including Form 8 (Annual Statement) and Form 11 (Annual Return).

LLP Agreement Form 8 & Form 11 Annual Compliance Conversion Advisory
🚀

Startup Advisory

Our startup advisory covers DPIIT recognition, valuation for funding rounds, ESOP structuring, investor due diligence support, financial modelling, and pitch deck financials. We also help you navigate Section 80-IAC tax exemptions and Startup India benefits.

DPIIT Recognition Startup India Tax Exemption ESOP Advisory Financial Modelling Due Diligence
📁

ROC Filings & Corporate Compliance

Maintain your company's good standing with MCA through timely ROC filings. We handle annual return filing (MGT-7), financial statement filing (AOC-4), board meeting compliance, Director KYC, charge filings, and maintenance of statutory registers and minutes books.

MGT-7 / AOC-4 Director KYC Board Meetings Statutory Registers
💼

Accounting Services

Accurate, timely, and insightful accounting services for complete financial visibility and informed decision-making

📚

Bookkeeping Services

Accurate books are the foundation of sound financial management. Our bookkeeping services include day-to-day transaction recording, bank reconciliation, accounts payable/receivable management, and compliance with Accounting Standards — using Tally Prime, QuickBooks, Zoho Books, and more.

Tally Prime QuickBooks Zoho Books Bank Reconciliation AP / AR Management
👥

Payroll Processing

We handle complete payroll — salary computation, PF, ESI and PT deductions, payslip generation, monthly remittances, annual Form 16 for employees, and full & final settlement calculations. Fully compliant with all applicable labour laws.

PF / ESI / PT Form 16 (Employees) Payslip Generation Full & Final Settlement
📊

Financial Statements Preparation

We prepare complete, auditable financial statements — Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Account, Cash Flow Statements, and Notes to Accounts — in accordance with applicable Accounting Standards (AS / Ind AS), ready for statutory audit, bank submissions, and investor review.

AS / Ind AS P&L & Balance Sheet Cash Flow Statement Notes to Accounts
📈

MIS Reporting

Transform your financial data into actionable business intelligence. We design customised MIS reports — monthly P&L analysis, cash flow tracking, budget vs actual comparisons, key financial ratios, and KPI dashboards — to help you make faster, more informed business decisions.

Monthly MIS Reports Budget vs Actual KPI Dashboards Cash Flow Forecast
